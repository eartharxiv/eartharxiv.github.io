Original EarthArXiv Logo The EarthArXiv logo was created by Andrew Cross. The logo is available via the Creative Commons CC-BY license with acknowledgment to Andrew. You can also give him a follow on Twitter. This original logo uses a font “Constantia” that is part of Microsoft/MS Office, which may not be available to users on Linux-based computers. If you have problems with this version consider the updated logos below.
